About Me

I’m Chase Jarvis, an IT specialist and full-stack web developer based in Warsaw, Poland. I bring 3+ years of hands-on Level 1/2 support and on-site/remote operations from Cirque du Soleil’s Touring Show Division, where I kept global productions running in high-pressure environments.

My day-to-day spans Windows 10/11 user support with ServiceNow, Active Directory/Entra ID, Intune, Office 365, and Cisco Meraki. I automate repetitive work with Ansible and PowerShell, including POS fleet updates. On every new city stop, I built complete site infrastructure — racks, switches, Wi-Fi, miles of Cat5/fiber, and ISP coordination — then supported laptops, servers, printers, and POS systems on show days.

Outside ops, I design accessible, fast websites in HTML5, CSS, and JavaScript and collaborate clearly with international teams — translating complex issues into human-friendly language. I’m originally from the U.S., now settled in Poland with my wife; I’m open to hybrid/remote roles and relocation for the right fit.
